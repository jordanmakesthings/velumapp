FAQ

Questions & answers.

What exactly is Velum?

Velum is one place for the practices that actually reach the patterns — breathwork, tapping, bilateral, somatic touch, meditation, guided journaling, and courses, bound together into a daily practice. It's built for people who've done the surface work and want something that goes deeper.

How is this different from Calm or Headspace?

Calm and Headspace give you ten minutes of mindfulness, then hand you back to the same life. Velum is active — tapping, bilateral, somatic — and it works at the level where the patterns actually live, so the change stays after the session ends instead of evaporating the moment you close the app.

What happens after the free trial?

No credit card needed to start. Your free 7-day trial gives you full access — including the AI custom-rewiring-audio generator. At the end of the trial you'll see your plan options inside the app — annual, monthly, or lifetime. Cancel before your trial ends and you're never charged.

Do I need any experience?

Velum is built for people who already know the vocabulary — who've done therapy, read the books, tried the modalities. You won't be starting from zero. But every tool still guides you step by step, so nothing assumes you'll know what to do.

Can I use Velum on my phone?

Yes — and it's designed for it. Velum is a progressive web app optimised for mobile. Open it in Safari or Chrome, tap "Add to Home Screen," and it works exactly like a native app on iOS and Android. No app store required.

Is this a replacement for therapy?

No. Velum is a wellness and educational tool, not a mental health treatment. The practices inside are not a substitute for professional care. If you're in crisis, please reach out to a qualified professional.
