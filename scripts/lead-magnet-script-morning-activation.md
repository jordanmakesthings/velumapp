# The Morning Activation
**Breath + Identity Install · 7 minutes**
**Velum lead magnet · recorded by Jordan**

---

## Recording notes

- Pace: slower than feels natural. ~110-120 wpm.
- Silence: pauses are marked `[pause: Ns]`. Trust them. Count internally.
- Tone: warm, grounded, present. Not breathy. Not "meditation voice." Sound like you do on YouTube.
- Two takes. The second is almost always the keeper.
